## What is Blynt?

**Blynt** is a personal finance application built for people who want to **manage their money without friction**.

Instead of filling out forms or selecting categories, Blynt lets you track income and expenses using **natural language**, the same way you think about money.

> _“Spent 20 on food yesterday”_  
> _“Got paid today”_

Blynt automatically understands and structures the information.

---

## Key features

- Natural language expense and income tracking
- AI-powered automatic categorization
- **Zero-based budgeting**
- Virtual envelope system
- Real-time balance updates
- **Daily ideal spending estimate** until the end of the month

---

## Why Blynt is different

Most finance apps force you to adapt to their system.  
Blynt adapts to **how you think**.

- Less friction, more clarity
- No financial expertise required
- Minimal and focused interface
- Strong privacy-first approach
